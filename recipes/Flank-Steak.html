What to Serve With Marinated Flank Steak

Pair your marinated flank steak with a side salad, grilled or roasted veggies, or something starchy (such as mashed potatoes or French fries). In need of a little inspiration? Check out our collection of the 20 Best Side Dishes for Steak. Here are a few of the recipes you'll find:

Marinade

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steak

  1. (1 1/2-pound) flank steak
